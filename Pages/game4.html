How to Play
  • You start with 2 minutes (120 seconds) on the timer
  • You must complete all 10 rooms to win the game
  • Read the NOTE on the wall - this tells you the rules for the current room
  • Each room has exactly ONE correct door
  • Choose the wrong door and you'll be sent back to Room 1
  • Complete a new room (one you haven't reached before) to gain 60 seconds
  • Time Bonus: You get 5 points for every 30 seconds remaining when you finish
